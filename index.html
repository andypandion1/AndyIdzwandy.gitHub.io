B2B Catering Services

DD Lunch provides corporations with a simple to use catering platform. Corporations can cater healthy meals for their employees to promote a healthy lifestyle within the company.

Who Are We

At its inception in 2014, Fruitso-Mania (now DD Lunch) specialised and grew to become a prominent figure in providing fresh yet affordable fruits and juices to consumers. We have since expanded to provide bulk-catering services for corporations with large numbers of workers in many industries including but not limited to construction, engineering, and automotive.
